Some hints about input:
  • The "apostrophe" can be expressed with two characters: ' and ’
  • The aspiration character may be expressed both by unicode upper h (ʰ) and just "h", so for example both "pʰ" and "ph" will work
  • The tones may be expressed by the following:
    • Rising: /
    • Falling: \
    • Rising-falling: ^
    • Falling-rising: ˇ or \/ (backslash-slash)
    • High: ¯ or -
    • Low: _
  • Other characters have to be entered exactly for now, but this may change in the future.
